Project Brief

Website/Resume

Background

Why am I doing this project

I am completing this project to highlight the skills I have learned as an aspiring Web Developer. This will be a live project that serves as my resume. This gives potential employers a chance to see what I can do, other projects I have completed/been a part of, education, and work experience.

Why is this necessary?

Every job I have applied to in my adult life has required that I submit some form of resume, and now the transition into tech, often a website link. If I could showcase things I have done previously and my resume in the same place, it could make me a more viable candidate vs. someone who does not have their work showcased.

What need is this project meeting?

This project is meeting my personal need to produce a project that showcases my abilities to potential employers.

Who is the target audience?

Potential employers in the technology field, hiring managers, teachers, peers
